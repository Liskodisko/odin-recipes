Overnight Oats

Description

A quick and easy recipe for a healthy breakfast.

Recipe posted on Allrecipes by sonjagroset

Image by Dotdash Meredith Food Studios

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup rolled oats
  • 2 teaspoons honey
  • 2 teaspoons chia seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon grounded cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup fresh berries

Steps

  1. Combine milk, yogurt, oats, honey, chia seeds, and cinnamon in a 1/2-pint jar with a lid; cover and shake until combined.
  2. Fold in berries.
  3. Cover and refrigerate for 8 hours to overnight.
